Hobbies

I often consider my occupation as my biggest hobby, as I enjoy work that is challenging and motivational. But I also have a few other actual hobbies that I occasionally find time for.

  • Sailing - My most favorite activity. We enjoy sailing our hobie tandem island kayaks around the area
  • Biking - Although I used to do this more often as a leisure activity, I still enjoy getting out to get some exercise on the local bike trails, along with an occasional commute to work
  • Hiking - I try to get my family outside when the opportunity presents itself, and often times the easiest thing to do is go for a hike. With plenty of options in the greater Seattle area, we never seem to run out of choices!
  • Soccer - I don't really play the game as much as I watch it; both of my daughters are players though. It is fun to go out to a park and kick the ball around. And I enjoy watching my girls play on their teams as well.
