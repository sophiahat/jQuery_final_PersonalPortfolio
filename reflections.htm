Studying Web Development

This is a unique page which is more of a blog about my current studies of web development. Although I'm fairly new to the program, I have enjoyed working through the material in the Pace-It program at Edmonds Community College. I find the process of moving through the material in a natural progression through the theory and technologies to be challenging and rewarding as well as thorough. There are a lot of resources out there for learning the material, but I don't think there are many that are as well thought out while being so accessible.

So far the material that has been the most enlightening to me is related to the planning and designing stages. I've been involved with other types of work where I'm called upon to create a work order and estimate cost, and I find the material related to these topics most helpful to me. I've had some experience with HTML, CSS and Javascript, so the material associated with those technologies have been a great review and have also filled in some gaps along the way. I look forward to learning new material as I feel that I'm fast approaching the extent of my current knowledge...
